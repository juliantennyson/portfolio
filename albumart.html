for the intro to typography class at western washington university, i created an interactive infographic displaying the different variations in colored vinyl records.

the poster presented a unique issue, because I wanted it to convey a lot of information while still being visually stimulating. the cover page printed on acetate creates an interesting visual texture while affording the audience to interact with it and see what is underneath.
