Dr. Magnuson's Shit For Gardening is a reimagining of a product system and point of purchase.

It is aimed at using sustainable materials and no-nonsense branding to attract future-minded patrons to think about the impact the products they use might have.

The point of purchase was hand made using recycled materials, making sure to emphasize the process in the final piece. This specific POP would be a display at the point of sale to exhibit everything Dr. Magnuson's has to offer.
